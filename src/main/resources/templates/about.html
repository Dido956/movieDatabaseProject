About us

Hello there! I'm Daniel Lazarov, and I'm excited to welcome you to my personal movie database website. This website is a labor of love that combines my passion for movies with my enthusiasm for technology.

The Idea

The idea for this movie database came to life as I found myself constantly exploring new films, old classics, and hidden gems. I wanted a space where I could not only keep track of my favorite movies but also share my recommendations with others. That's how this project began, and it has been an incredible journey.

The Website

This database is a cinematic journey. From timeless classics to the latest releases, you'll find a diverse collection of movies. Each movie entry is curated with care. You can find thoughts, ratings, and recommendations.
